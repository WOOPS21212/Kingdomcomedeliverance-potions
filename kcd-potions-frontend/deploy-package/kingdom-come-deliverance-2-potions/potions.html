Potions

Aesop

Ingredients:

  • Belladonna x1
  • Boar’s Tusk x1
  • Comfrey x2

Increase horsemanship and houndmaster by 5 and makes it easier to sneak up on animals for 24 hours

Brewing Steps:

  1. Add 2 Comfrey to the mortar, grind, and pour into the cauldron
  2. Add 1 Boar’s Tusk to the cauldron
  3. Boil for 2 turns
  4. Add 1 Belladonna to the mortar, grind, and pour into the cauldron
  5. Prepare phial and distill

Aqua Vitalis

Base: Water

Ingredients:

  • Dandelion x1
  • Marigold x1

Lose 50% less Health and slows bleeding by 50% for 10 minutes

Brewing Steps:

  1. Add 2 Dandelion to the cauldron
  2. Boil for 1 turn
  3. Add 1 Marigold to the mortar, grind, and pour into the cauldron
  4. Boil for 2 turns
  5. Prepare phial and distill

Enhanced Effects:

At higher quality, further reduces health loss and bleeding rate

Where to Find:

Can be purchased from Aranka in the Nomads’ Camp, Herbalist Barnaby north of Trosky Castle, or the apothecary in Kuttenberg
